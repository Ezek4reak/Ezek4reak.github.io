About Me

How it all started eleven (11+) years ago

The journey all started in my first semester year one of my 2 years undergraduate diploma in computer science back in 2011 when my lecturer introduced us to Qbasic, he was explaining a program to add two numbers then I asked him a question, “why would one go through all these stress to write a code just to add two number when you can just use the calculator?” My lecturer laughed and said something that changed me forever. He said “the calculators, the telephone and even the computers are all powered by code in the background and people like me wrote the codes”.

I became so curious, prior to that we think of a computer as one magic box from the devil, of course I came from a background where I’ve never had access to a computer. In that same first semester we were introduced to Google search where we can find answers to almost any question and that again is a game changer, but because access to internet is very very expensive I couldn’t do much at that time until I finished the school in 2013 and started working as an inventory management assistant, I can now afford internet access to do research, I started finding the best way to learn programming and I stumble on Harvard’s CS50. Prof. David J. Malan is actually a God sent, he gave us a solid foundation in computational thinking and not just programming.

After the CS50 I took Three certificate courses in Udemy on Core PHP, PHP MVC, and Javascript and obtained certificate of completion.

Because I was working as inventory management assistant and we were using different excel sheets to keep records, my first task is to build an inventory management system using PHP MVC design pattern, although I couldn’t convince the management of the company who doesn’t want to spend money on hosting and daily internet required to use an online system.

I have worked on some individual project which some of them are not live anymore as the owner cannot maintain the online presence. Some of such projects are online cash register for the accountant of Juba Construction limited.

One of my live project now is United Nation Rescue Services Volunteer biometric system and blog.

In all these i have been working as a freelancer, but now i am ready to take up more challange where i can grow by working with more experience Developers. so yes, i am looking to be hired as a junior Developer.
